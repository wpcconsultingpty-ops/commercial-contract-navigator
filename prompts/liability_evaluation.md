# Liability Evaluation Prompt

## Task
Assess the contract's liability clause from a small business commercial perspective.

Classify the liability position as one of the following:
- Acceptable
- Watch
- High Risk

Your assessment must be based on commercial impact and market practice only.

You must NOT:
- provide legal advice
- assess legal enforceability
- interpret legislation or regulation

## Commercial Benchmarks

### Acceptable
- Liability is capped at fees paid under the contract or required insurance limits.

### Watch
- Liability is capped at a multiple of fees (for example 2x or 3x the fees).

### High Risk
- Liability is unlimited, uncapped, or not clearly defined.

Respond using the following structure only:

Risk Level:  

Why This Matters Commercially:  
Commercial Context:
